In this work, I have studied and replicated attacks on the privacy of a Machine Learning model in a distributed context. It has been demonstrated that averaging gradients over different iterations or different images does not safeguard user privacy in Federated Learning applications. Therefore, a faithful reconstruction of high-resolution images has been achieved from the knowledge of gradients.

You can see my full presentation in the link above.
