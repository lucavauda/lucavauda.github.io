Why have a blog?

Writing is one of those skills you can get better at just by doing it. It’s not just about putting words on a page; it’s about clarifying your own thoughts. When I write, it forces me to figure out what I really think. Sometimes, writing is the only way I can make sense of what's in my head.

This blog is a place for me to do just that — make sense of things. Here, you’ll find my thoughts on cybersecurity, machine learning, and maybe a mix of other topics that catch my interest. I’m still figuring it out, and that’s part of the fun.

So, if you’re curious too, come along for the ride. Let’s see where this takes us.
